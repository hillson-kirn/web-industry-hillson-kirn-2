Web Design And Development

Web Design and Development is an up-and-coming industry. How can you get in on the action? Click below to learn more!

Job Overview

Web developers and designers create and maintain websites, as well as test things like interface and navigation for usability. There is generally no on-the-job training or work experience in a related field required. It is a very flexible and diverse job when it comes to opportunities, while some developers are self-employed, others work in various industries like advertising or consulting.

Money

The median pay for all web designers or developers was $73,660, with an hourly wage of $35.46 an hour (2019). The job outlook for the next 10 years is expected to go up 8%, which is much faster than average. This growth is mostly due to the increase in devices that can access and view websites in the past few years. WIth this pay combined with the flexible and accessible terms and requirements to become a web designer, it makes it look like a very good and plausible job.
